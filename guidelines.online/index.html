How does the app actually work?

Upload your logo package (ZIP or individual files) and the app instantly builds a complete one-page brand guideline. It extracts your colors, identifies fonts, organizes assets, and generates sections like logo usage, color palette, typography, and DO’s & DON’Ts—automatically.

What file formats do you support?

Is best to upload vector files like SVG, EPS, and AI files—or just drop in a ZIP folder containing all your assets. The app sorts and processes everything in the background, so you don’t need to prepare or organize files manually.

Can I customize the style guide?

Yes, absolutely. Every section is fully editable—colors, fonts, spacing, imagery, examples, and text. You can reorder blocks, rewrite content, add new sections, and update design elements at any time.
